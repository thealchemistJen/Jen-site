Artist Biography

Jen Folsom is a creator and educator currently living in Aniane, France. Prior to relocating to France, Jen was a professor of design and photography at the University of Northern Colorado and Front Range Community College. She holds her MFA in Photography from the Savannah College of Art and Design and her work continues to be exhibited internationally.
